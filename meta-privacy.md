## 1. Introduction

This application ("App") is developed and operated by an independent developer for educational, testing, and API exploration purposes.

The App integrates with services provided by Meta Platforms, including Facebook, Instagram, the Meta Marketing API, and the Facebook Graph API.

This Privacy Policy explains how data is accessed, used, stored, and protected.

---

## 2. Permissions Requested

The App requests the following permissions:

### ads_management
Used to:
- Create and manage advertising campaigns
- Configure ad sets and ads
- Manage ad creatives and budgets

### ads_read
Used to:
- Retrieve advertising performance metrics
- Access campaign insights and analytics

### business_management
Used to:
- Access business asset information
- View associated ad accounts and assets
- Review business structure details

### pages_read_engagement
Used to:
- Read Page metadata
- Access engagement metrics
- View public Page content and insights

## 3. Purpose of Data Use

Data accessed through the above permissions is used strictly for:

- Learning and understanding API behavior
- Testing marketing and advertising workflows
- Development and debugging purposes
- Exploring campaign and creative configurations

The App is not a commercial advertising service and does not manage campaigns for third parties.

## 6. User Data Deletion

Users may remove the App and associated data through Facebook:

1. Go to Facebook → Settings & Privacy  
2. Click Settings  
3. Select Apps and Websites  
4. Choose Your App Name  
5. Click Remove  

Alternatively, users may request deletion by emailing:

**Email:** reachsn100@gmail.com  
**Subject:** Data Deletion Request  

All valid deletion requests will be processed within 30 days.
